Flower Care Tips

Watering Your Flowers

Ensure your flowers receive fresh water daily. Cut the stems at an angle for better water absorption.

Keeping Flowers Fresh

Keep your flowers in a cool place, away from direct sunlight or heat sources to extend their life.

Orchid Care

Orchids need indirect sunlight and should only be watered once a week. Use orchid-specific fertilizers for best results.
